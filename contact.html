我们欢迎更多的科研工作者参与到此系列报告中，分享最新的研究成果，课题综述，技巧方法等。如果您有意愿做报告，或者想为我们推荐报告人，请联系陈俊彦或尹富纲。

加入讨论班或组织团队，请联系张宝羽。

报告网站维护问题，请联系丁兆宸。

If you are a young group theorist or graph theorist, and want to give a talk, please drop an email to Junyan Chen or Fu-Gang Yin. We would also love to hear any suggestions you have for speakers. Don't worry if you have no new results -- we also warmly welcome any survey, or even a method that you find helpful, on any related topic.

To express your interest of participation (including joining our organization team), feel free to get in touch with Hong Yi Huang.

For any issue about this website, please contact Zhaochen Ding.
